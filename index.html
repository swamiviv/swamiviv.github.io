Swami Sankaranarayanan

I am a Postdoctoral Associate at MIT working with Phillip Isola and Antonio Torralba

I was a Research Scientist at Butterfly Network, working with Nathan Silberman and was an integral part of the team that developed the ML-based Auto Bladder Volume tool among others.

During my Ph.D. I was advised by Prof. Rama Chellappa. I have explored different aspects of deep learning representations from the point of view of robustness and invariance. I have worked on several topics such as Semantic Segmentation, Domain Adaptation, Adversarial learning, Multi task learning and Facial recognition systems.

I am interested in developing learning algorithms that are not only certain about knowing things but are also aware of their own uncertainties i.e. to know when they don't know.
